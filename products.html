Export Logistics

Packaging & Shipping

We provide secure, export-grade packaging customised to our clients' exact requirements — from vacuum-sealed cartons to bulk FIBC jumbo bags.

Vacuum Bags (Max 25 kg)

Vacuum-sealed bags of up to 25 kg packed inside export-grade corrugated cartons. Maximises shelf life and prevents moisture ingress during sea transport. Suitable for blanched and processed peanuts.

Jute / PP Woven Bags

Traditional 25 kg / 50 kg New Jute or polypropylene woven bags — the industry standard for raw and in-shell peanut shipments. Cost-effective and widely accepted across all markets.

FIBC Jumbo Bags

1000 kg / 1250 kg Flexible Intermediate Bulk Containers for industrial buyers. Securely palletized for efficient container loading, reducing handling costs for large volume orders.

  Independent Pre-Shipment Inspection

Every shipment is independently inspected by internationally recognised surveyors (SGS, Geo-Chem, or equivalent) prior to container stuffing — ensuring your shipment is exactly as specified.
