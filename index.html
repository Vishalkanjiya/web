About ATYA GLOBAL

India's Most Trusted Peanut Export House

Based in Morbi, Rajkot, Gujarat, India — at the heart of India's peanut belt — ATYA GLOBAL is a dedicated peanut export house that connects the world's finest peanuts to buyers in 20+ countries. We partner with India's most reputable, GMP-certified processing units to ensure every shipment meets the highest international quality benchmarks.

To serve our global clientele year-round, we have established strategic sourcing partnerships with premium suppliers in Argentina and Brazil, allowing us to offer every peanut variety, origin, and specification under a single, trusted partnership.

  • Rigorous quality audits at every partner processing unit
  • In-house aflatoxin & moisture testing before every shipment
  • India, Argentina & Brazil origins — one trusted partner
  • SGS / Geo-Chem independent pre-shipment inspection
