Our Story

About ATYA GLOBAL

A multi-national agricultural export house built on quality, trust, and the world's finest peanuts.

Our Vision

Setting the Global Benchmark for Peanut Excellence

ATYA GLOBAL is a dedicated peanut export house headquartered in Morbi, Rajkot, Gujarat, India — at the epicentre of the world's finest groundnut-growing region. Our mission is to be the single most reliable peanut sourcing partner for food manufacturers, traders, and importers anywhere in the world.

Operating under our flagship brand ATYA AGRO, we specialize in the sourcing, quality control, and export of premium peanuts. We work hand-in-hand with India's most reputable, GMP-certified processing partners to ensure that every shipment meets the most stringent international quality standards.

To guarantee year-round supply of any variety, our strategic sourcing network spans India, Argentina, and Brazil — three of the world's top peanut-producing nations — giving our global customers a single contact for all their peanut requirements.

Our Values

Why Buyers Choose ATYA GLOBAL

Zero-Defect Quality

Every batch is sourced from HACCP and ISO-certified processing partners and undergoes strict pre-shipment inspection by independent surveyors like SGS.

Multi-Origin Supply

With sourcing from India, Argentina, and Brazil, we guarantee year-round supply of any peanut variety, grade, or specification your business requires.

Trusted Partnerships

Long-term relationships with importers in 20+ countries built on consistent quality, transparent communication, and on-time, hassle-free delivery.

Technology-Backed Sourcing

We only partner with processing units equipped with Bühler optical sorters, gravity separators, and modern aflatoxin testing labs to guarantee 99.9% purity.

Ethical Sourcing

We enforce fair-trade and sustainability practices at farm and processing level across all our supply chain origins for responsible, traceable exports.

Full Compliance

All exports are fully compliant with EU, FDA, and international food safety regulations. Certified under APEDA, FSSAI, ISO 9001:2015, HACCP, RCMC, GST, and UDYAM.

We invest heavily in optical sorting technology and multi-origin partnerships because we understand that our customers rely on ATYA GLOBAL to protect their brand's reputation."

Executive Leadership

Under the vision of Founder Vishal Kanjiya, ATYA GLOBAL has grown from a Gujarat-based exporter to a truly global agricultural enterprise, with buyers spanning Europe, the Middle East, Southeast Asia, and beyond.

His philosophy — that the peanut industry deserves the same precision, technology, and integrity found in any premium manufactured good — has driven every business decision, from equipment investment to supplier selection.

Responsibility

Commitment to Sustainability

As a major force in global agriculture, we take our responsibility to the planet and farming communities deeply seriously.
